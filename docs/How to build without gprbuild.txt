Choose your platform by copying the contents of either main/nonwindows or main/windows to main/

Choose whether your compiler and OS support Unicode by copying the contents of either main/unicode or main/nonunicode to main/

Copy the contents of dictionary/ to main/

Now all required files are in main

Manually compile and bind the following programs (e.g., "gnatmake <program_name>"")

  makedict
  makeefil
  makeewds
  makeinfl
  makestem
  sorter
  words

Execute rebuild.sh or rebuild.cmd
